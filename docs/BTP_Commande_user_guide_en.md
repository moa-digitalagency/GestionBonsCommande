# BTP Commande - User Guide

Welcome to the **BTP Commande** platform. This guide accompanies you in the daily use of the tool.

---

## 1. Dashboard

Upon login, you access a summary view:
*   **Orders in Progress:** Drafts and orders awaiting submission.
*   **To Validate:** (If you are a Validator) Orders requiring your approval.
*   **Statistics:** Total number of orders and committed budget.

---

## 2. Order Management

### 2.1 Create a Purchase Order (PO)
1.  Menu **"Purchase Orders"** > **"New"**.
2.  **General Information:**
    *   **Site:** Select the relevant project.
    *   **Desired Date:** Expected delivery date.
    *   **Notes:** Instructions for the deliverer or internal note.
3.  **Adding Items:**
    *   Click on **"Add a line"**.
    *   Fill in the description (e.g., "CPJ45 Cement").
    *   Indicate the quantity and unit (Bag, Ton, Pallet...).
    *   *Note:* Translation to Arabic will be automatic upon saving.
4.  **Save:** The order is saved as a **Draft**.

### 2.2 Submit for Validation
Once the draft is complete:
1.  Check the totals.
2.  Click on **"Submit"**.
3.  The order status changes to **Submitted** and notifies your manager. You can no longer modify it.

### 2.3 Validate / Reject (Managers)
1.  Open an order in **Submitted** status.
2.  Check the content.
3.  **Actions:**
    *   ‚úÖ **Validate:** The order becomes official (**Validated**).
    *   ‚ùå **Reject:** A window opens to enter the reason for refusal. The order reverts to **Draft** for correction.

### 2.4 Generate PDF
On a **Validated** order:
1.  Click the **"Download PDF"** button.
2.  The official document (with logo and signature) is generated.

### 2.5 Share (WhatsApp / Email)
Use the quick share buttons to send the PO directly to the supplier:
*   **WhatsApp:** Opens a conversation with the pre-filled message and PO link.

---

## 3. The Dictionary (Lexique)

BTP Commande integrates a technical translation engine.

### 3.1 Search for a Term
1.  Menu **"Dictionary"**.
2.  Type a word in French (or Arabic).
3.  The system displays exact matches and aliases.

### 3.2 Suggest a Translation
If a term is missing:
1.  Click on **"Suggest a term"**.
2.  Fill in the French word and its proposed translation.
3.  Your suggestion will be reviewed by an administrator.

---

## 4. My Account

*   **Change Language:** Click on the flag at the top right (FR/EN/AR). The interface adapts automatically (RTL for Arabic).
*   **Logout:** To close your session securely.
